Biography

Adele, formerly known as Adele Laurie Blue Adkins, is an extremely talented Singer-Songwriter with a gentle soul. She was born in London, England on May 5, 1988 to parents Penny Adkins and Marc Evans. She was raised by her mother and relocated to various parts of Englad throughout her early childhood. She developed a profound interest in music at a very early age and began singing at 4 years old. Many of the cities she resided in helped mold her career and are even cited in many of songs. Her artistic career took off in England at the age of 19 and later made a breakthrough in the United status with her second album titled 21. She became a sensation in US and since then has released a subsequent album titled 25.
